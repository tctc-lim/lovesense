About Our Company

WHO WE ARE?

We are an international company that offers virtual counseling to individuals and couples. We work with clients facing a wide range of mental health challenges. Our counselors are trained and experienced in various schools of treatment concerning relationship, depression, etc. Our purpose is to help treat people through a relaxed virtual dialog talk therapy.
PLEASE NOTE: We are NOT a medical or psychiatric clinic and do NOT provide clinical services.

OUR THERAPY APPROACHES

We have several counselors who use various therapy approaches. We primarily use a person-centered approach with tools and techniques from Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Emotion-Focused Therapy (EFT) and Task-Centered Competency Based Therapy (TCBT). As much as possible, we adapt the therapy to the client's needs using empirically tested Feedback Informed Therapy (FIT) techniques..
